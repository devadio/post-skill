Quick Guide

Quick Steps

If this is your first time using Google Sheet automation, follow these easy steps:

  1. Enable Google Sheet automation.
    Generate your token and IDs at post.devad.io/app/profile/settings.
  2. Select your posting frequency in the Automation section.
  3. Click SAVE & RUN SYNC.

Watch

The log will show the result, and Action? will be updated based on the output.

Tips

  • Use a sheet tab named post.
  • Do not edit other fields if you want the sheet to keep working correctly.
  • Keep Row 1 headers and dropdown menus unchanged.
  • Your AI agent role, or your own role, is to fill only these fields with your data: Promo Link B2:B, Title C2:C, Caption D2:D, Media URL E2:E, Media Type F2:F, Action G2:G.
  • To enable Google Sheet automation, open the new menu named POST.devad.io at the top of the Google Sheet, right after Help.
  • This version currently supports these Creative Types only: image_manual, video_manual, carousel_manual.
  • If you want the full version, please contact us.
